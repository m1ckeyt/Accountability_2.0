Accomplish Anything.

Accomplish goals with friends and family by you side. Accountability is here to help you organize goals, remain on track, and make a positive impact on your life, or someone elses.

"If you want to achieve your goals, help others achieve their goals." -Zig Ziglar

Whether you're training for a triathalon or studying for a big test, goals need commitment and dedication. You're more likely to succeed with constant reminders of milestones that need to be met and by having friends also accountable for your deadlines. Accountability is the place to find all these resources so you can achieve your goals, and help others achieve their's.

How it Works

Set Goals

Create a timeline of milestones in order to achieve the main goal.

Pledge to Succeed

Pledge to donate to a charity of choice. Friends and family will be notified of your journey and help root you on!

Stay on Track

Frequent reminders of deadlines will be sent to you to ensure that you are on track. A progress chart can be shared with friends and family.

Make a Difference

Achieve your dreams! With hardwork and dedication anything can be achieved. But sometimes life doesn't always work out as planned. If goals are postponed for a future date, the pledged donation will help benefit the lives of others. By joining Accountability, you'll be making a positive difference in one way or another.

Let's Keep in Touch!

Interested in Accountability? Have recommendations? Want to tell us a funny joke? Subscribe to us and we'll add you to our mailing list for future updates. We're holding you Accountable!
